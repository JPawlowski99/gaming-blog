Marvel’s Spider-Man 2 [PS5]

A sequel to the acclaimed 2018 game, featuring Peter Parker and Miles Morales as they face new threats in New York City. You can play both Parker and Morales, each with their own unique abilities and storylines, as you face off against a new threat that involves Kraven the Hunter and Venom. The combat is fluid and dynamic, allowing you to unleash a variety of web-based attacks and gadgets, as well as the unique abilities of each Spider-Man. The story is engaging and emotional, exploring the themes of friendship, family, and responsibility.

Reviews Games

Apex Legends Mobile

This is a new mobile FPS game that is based on the hit PC and console game Apex Legends. The game is a team-based battle royale game that features unique characters with different abilities and skills. The game also has a ping system that lets you communicate with your teammates without voice chat, and a respawn system that lets you revive your fallen allies. The game is praised for its fast-paced and fluid gameplay, its colorful and stylized graphics, and its innovative and fun mechanics. Apex Legends Mobile is currently in beta testing in some regions, but it is expected to launch globally later this year.

9.5
Elden Ring

This is the highly anticipated collaboration between FromSoftware, the creators of Dark Souls and Sekiro, and George R.R. Martin, the author of A Song of Ice and Fire. The game is an open-world action RPG that combines the challenging combat and rich lore of FromSoftware’s games with the epic fantasy and world-building of Martin’s novels. The game lets you create your own character, explore a vast and varied land, fight against fearsome enemies and bosses, and uncover the secrets of the Elden Ring, a mysterious artifact that shapes the world’s history and destiny. The game also features online multiplayer, where you can cooperate or compete with other players in your journey.

9.2
Disco Elysium

This is a critically acclaimed RPG that puts you in the role of a detective who has to solve a murder case in a dystopian city. The game is praised for its deep and branching story, its unique skill system, its witty and immersive dialogue, and its stunning art style. The game gives you a lot of freedom and choices in how you approach the investigation, as well as how you shape your character’s personality, morality, and ideology. The game also features a dynamic soundtrack that adapts to your actions and mood. Disco Elysium is one of the best PC games of 2023, and it has won several awards, including four BAFTAs and three Game Awards.

8.7
Mass Effect Legendary Edition

This is a remastered collection of the original Mass Effect trilogy, one of the most beloved sci-fi RPG series of all time. The collection includes all three games, as well as over 40 DLCs, enhanced graphics, improved gameplay, and new customization options. The collection lets you relive the epic saga of Commander Shepard, a human soldier who has to save the galaxy from an ancient threat known as the Reapers. The collection also lets you experience the rich and diverse characters, worlds, and stories that make up the Mass Effect universe. Mass Effect Legendary Edition is one of the best PC games of 2023 for fans of sci-fi and RPGs.
